Turn the reconciled evidence audit (11-AUDIT.md, from Plan 11-01) into a signed
v1.6 launch decision, hand off post-launch monitoring with named owners, and
point operators at the rollback/resume runbook.

checkpoint is the final task and gates the flip to GO.

launch sequence be documented in order with approvals, bounded stop conditions,
and rollback/resume notes, and that monitoring owners know first-run checks.
