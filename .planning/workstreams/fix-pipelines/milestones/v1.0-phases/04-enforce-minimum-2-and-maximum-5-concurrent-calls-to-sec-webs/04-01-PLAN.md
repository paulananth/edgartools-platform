<objective>
Add a 9 req/sec in-process rate limiter to sec_client.py and document the BOOTSTRAP_BATCH_CONCURRENCY safe operating range in CLAUDE.md.

Purpose: Prevent the warehouse ECS tasks from triggering SEC EDGAR's 10 req/sec per-IP rate limit by adding an in-process token-bucket limiter to every outbound SEC HTTP call. Simultaneously, give operators authoritative documentation on the safe concurrency range for bootstrap pipelines.

- `CLAUDE.md` — note in the Phased Pipeline > Key invariants section documenting `BOOTSTRAP_BATCH_CONCURRENCY` recommended range of 2–5 with current default of 3 as compliant
