Type: research
Status: resolved

## Question

How far back do definitive proxies remain useful for agent employment baselines
and multi-year officer sets, versus “latest proxy only”?

## Exit criteria

- Role of **baseline proxy** (may predate history band).
- Recommended history band (e.g. 5y) for agent GO.
- What older proxies are only Explore-tier.

## Answer

**Gist:** Always load the **latest definitive proxy ≤ W** (baseline may predate the history band) **plus** proxies with filing date ≥ **W − 5 years**. Older non-baseline proxies are Explore-only; do not require every proxy since 2013 for agent GO.
